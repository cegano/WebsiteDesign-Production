Instrument Rental or Purchase

SquareOne Music & Lessons is one of the largest providers of musical instrument rental in the U.S. Educator-approved and trusted by teachers, we have over 300 retail and affiliate locations with more than 150 educational representatives servicing schools across the country.

Renting an instrument is a great way for you or your child to try an instrument without needing to make a substantial up-front investment. If your child outgrows the instrument, changes to another instrument or it's time to upgrade past the beginner model, you can exchange your child's instrument for one of equal or greater value.

SquareOne Music & Lessons provides customers with several options with every instrument we carry. You can rent-to-own or purchase with in-store options and services. We also provide delivery for instruments and accessories for your convenience. Fill out our customer information form for more information or come to one of our locations today!

Rent-to-Own

Did you know all of our rental customers are eligible for special purchase options? When you participate in the rent-to-own program, you are actually working towards paying off your instrument!

Exchange

With our rental program you get the chance to try it out without committing to a purchase. If you decide later it's not your instrument, you may exchange it for another instrument of equal or greater value throughout the life of the agreement!

Upgrade

Beginner level instruments are a lot like “easy mode” in a video game. When you upgrade, a step-up instrument is “intermediate” and professional instruments are for “experts.” If a student has truly mastered the beginner level instrument, then a step-up instrument is the next move.
